Maths Olympiad Resources

This website is to help teachers and students who want to start learning Maths Olympiads. It's hard to bridge the gap between math from highschool, and the math experiences you need to be able to solve probles in olympiads. The main hope of this site is to share resources as well as experiences others have had, so that organizations hoping to train the next generation of math olympiad students, as well as students who want to learn about olympiads know what resouces to look for and help them study.

Who is this website for?

This website is for anyone interested in starting learning Maths Olympiads! Having said this, for those who are more experienced, please check other website listed under Other Useful Websites.
